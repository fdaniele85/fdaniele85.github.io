--- layout: page title: About me ---

Daniele Ferone obtained his Ph.D. in Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Napoli “Federico II” with a thesis on “The Shortest Path Tour Problem and its variants”. He spent a year as PostDoc Researcher at the Universitat Oberta de Catalunya, Barcelona, Spain, and he is currently working at University of Milano Bicocca in the MIND group on the project “Tourist tour planning models”. His main research interests are meta-heuristics, shortest path problems, and logistic optimization.
